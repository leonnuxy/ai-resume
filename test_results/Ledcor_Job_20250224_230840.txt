Description You are a System Solution Developer looking to contribute your expertise and enable projects by delivering and supporting technical solutions through application development on low-code platforms (e.g. Quickbase, Power BI). You have experience with development, troubleshooting support. We offer an industry-leading benefits package, ongoing employee training and development, team building and career growth opportunities. Apply today to join Ledcor Technical Services in Edmonton or Calgary, AB today! Learn more about working at Ledcor: Watch here . Essential Responsibilities: Understand and enhance business processes with the use of our low-code platforms. Development of applications: table building, field creation, forms, and relationships. Create reports and dashboards to provide operational metrics for business leaders. Critical assessment of user requests and the ability to discern their impact on existing functionality. Optimize and improve system setup and workflows. Troubleshoots and resolves issues brought up by users. Elevate skill sets of existing users through hands on training and guidance on best practices. Gather requirements for user requests in a clear and concise manner. Coordinate development, testing, acceptance, and handoff between users and development team. Qualifications: Strong problem-solving and analytical skills, with the ability to understand complex business requirements and translate them into technical solutions. Strong organizational skills, with the ability to manage multiple projects simultaneously and prioritize tasks effectively. Excellent communication and collaboration skills, with the ability to work effectively in a team environment as well as independently. Ability to write clear and concise technical documentation, including application specifications, user manuals, and training materials. Quickbase Expert Certification is an asset. Construction coordination background is an asset.
